Helief

Migraine is the 3rd most prevalent disease in the world. It affects 2% of the population worlwide chronically. (Source:  WHO). And more than 90% of sufferers are unable to work or function normally.

But yet, headache disorders are not perceived by the public as serious since they are mostly episodic, do not cause death, and are not contagious.

Therefore, doctors and healthcare public are not trained enough to help patients overcome their migraines, that can have many differents triggers and associated solutions.

But if you ever had a migraine, you know how painful and crippling it can be, and most patients are desperately looking for a solution.

User research

I made 1 survey (172 respondents) and conducted 2 users interviews of 1 hour with people who suffer regularly from migraines.

Problem statement

People who have at least one migraine per month

Need to find a way to record information about their migraine and lifestyle

So that they can identify patterns in their migraines and what could triggers them

Hypothesis statement

We believe that having a solution to record information about their migraines

Will achieve their goal of identifying patterns and triggers in order to reduce them

We will know we are right when our solution will help 50% of the users to identify what trigger their migraine with a percentage of confidence of 80%.

Meet Elena, our user persona

User scenario

How can Elena achieve her goals?

  • Elena will record information about her migraine
  • Elena will consult statistics about her migraines (symptoms, triggers etc.)
  • Elena will share her migraine statistics with her doctor

Where does Elena need to achieve this goal?

  • During the day, at work or at home
  • With her doctor when she has an appointment

User stories

As Elena, a user who currently has a migraine
I want to  record simple information about my migraine (level of intensity, medication, symptoms)
So that  I can remember the details later without any effort

As Elena, a user who currently has a migraine
I want to  complete all the details about my migraine (what happened before, extra details about the symptoms etc.)
So that  I can remember the details later without any effort

As Elena, a user who recorded at least one migraine
I want to  see statistics about my migraines and share them with my doctor
So that  we can identify together a pattern and find a possible solution

...and create a mid-fidelity prototype!

In the first version of my prototype, the user could enter information (medication taken, level of pain etc.) during the migraine. After testing this prototype on 2 people, I realized that people didn't want to spend time on their phone while they were in pain.

So I decided to simplify as much as possible the flow during the migraine, and I replaced it by a step-by-step form once the migraine is over. I tested it on 2 others people, and again, users complained because the form was pretty complicated and long to fill.

That's how I decided to simplify the flow once again, by using a unique screen after the end of the migraine. Users can enter more details in relevant categories (food, medicine etc.) .

Finally, I tested my last version on Maze on 5 people. This helped me to confirmed that the flow was correct.
