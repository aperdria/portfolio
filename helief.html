Migraine is the 3rd most prevalent disease in the world. It affects 2% of the population worlwide chronically. (Source:  WHO). And more than 90% of sufferers are unable to work or function normally

But yet, headache disorders are not perceived by the public as serious since they are mostly episodic, do not cause death, and are not contagious.

Therefore, doctors and healthcare public are not trained enough to help patients overcome their migraines, that can have many differents triggers and associated solutions.

But if you ever had a migraine, you know how painful and crippling it can be, and most patients are desperately looking for a solution.
